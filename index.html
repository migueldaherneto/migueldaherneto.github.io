About me

I'm a music composer / producer (born in Brazil, 1984), specialized in Midi/VST (virtual instruments) and sample libraries.
I'm able to create music of most syles, but my strengths are orchestral and hybrid ("game music").
Feel free to listen to some of my demos on this page!

Pricing/Methodology

Current rates are negotiable, starting at $100 per minute of (production-ready) music.

This can vary depending on the scope of the project. Once you describe your project with details, I will let you know how much I'm willing to charge for it. For more involved projects where I need to be an active participant, I might charge an hourly rate instead.


My services include:


- Constant communication with devs in order to create something cohesive
- Ability to create sketches to test in-game before commiting to an idea (which I might do at a cost if you're not commited to hiring me yet)
- Ability to work with programming tools for dynamic music if needed (I'm a senior developer/programmer)
- Ability to deliver production-ready tracks (mastering) mantaining proper/cohesive levels across the soundtrack (LUFS)
- Ability to work fast and make fast changes when needed (several years of experience with my DAW of choice, use of templates, proficiency with midi keyboard, etc)
