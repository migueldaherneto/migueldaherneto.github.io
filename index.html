About me

I'm a music composer / producer (born in Brazil, 1984), specialized in Midi/VST (virtual instruments) and sample libraries, with a focus for creating video-game OSTs.
I'm able to create music of most styles, but my strengths are orchestral and modern/oldschool/hybrid game music. My favored niche are RPGs and jRPGs.
As a sidenote, I'm also a software developer, with SOME knowledge of game development, but my experience of 10+ years has been mostly web, mobile and apps other than games. You can find more details about my technology skills on my linkedin page.
Feel free to listen to some of my demos on this page!

Pricing/Methodology

For the creation of production-ready music, current rates are negotiable, but start at a suggested rate of $100 per minute of music for less involved projects*.

* This can vary depending on the scope of the project. Once you describe your project with details, I will let you know how much I will charge for it. For more involved projects where I need to be an active participant, the rate per minute will be higher. The expected timeframe for actual payment can be discussed, but for large projects I do favor smaller monthly payments once the initial tracks are approved.


My services include:


- Brainstorming with development/artistic team in order to create something cohesive
- Ability to create sketches to test in-game before commiting to an idea (which I might do at a cost if you're not commited to hiring me yet)
- Ability to work with programming tools for dynamic music if needed (making use of my background as a software developer)
- Ability to deliver production-ready tracks (mastering) mantaining proper/cohesive levels across the soundtrack (LUFS)
- Ability to work fast and make fast changes when needed (several years of experience with my DAW of choice, use of templates, proficiency with midi keyboard, etc)
